    • PROCESS OF BUSINESS SETUP IN DUBAI FREE ZONE


    Determine the type of legal entity
    Firstly you have to determine the type of legal entity that you want to set up in the Dubai free zone. Depending on the particular free zone, it will vary.
    Trade name
    You can now choose your trade name for your company formation in Dubai free zone. Check for the permitted trade names in that free zone because there will be a chance that it is already registered.
    Business License
    Then apply for a business license there will be a list of different business licenses issued by the free zone and the type of business license depends on the activity of your business. It is necessary to submit the required documents when applying for a business license.
    Setup your office space
    Next, you have to set up the office space for your business formation in Dubai. You can buy or lease office space in the free zone. Calculate your employee’s total number and the type of activity then set up the office space
    Approval
    Then get the approval from the related authorities and register your business and get your business license. It will take some weeks to get done all this process and once you get the license you can start your business setup in Dubai.

    DIFFERENCE BETWEEN MAINLAND AND FREEZONE COMPANY FORMATION

    Dubai mainland and free zone company formation differ in many ways, including ownership, office space, and visa requirements.

      MAINLAND

    • If you are going to a company formation in Dubai mainland the UAE government has allowed for 100% ownership of the company. 
    • One can free do business anywhere in the UAE. That means the company can do business both inside and outside of the UAE. 
    • Office space requirement is a maximum of 140 sq. ft. on the UAE mainland.
    • There is not a kind of restrictions on visa eligibility. The visa issues are as per the company space.
    • Mainland companies have to take approvals from various government bodies.

      FREEZONE

    • Having a business setup in Dubai free zone you will get complete ownership of the company.
    • There are restrictions to the scope of business that is they are only allowed to do business with the particular free zone.
    • In a free zone company, there is no requirement for the company space. A company can be formed with or without premises.
    • There are some restrictions on visa eligibility in company formation in Dubai.
    • In a free zone company setup, there is no need for approvals from various government bodies.
